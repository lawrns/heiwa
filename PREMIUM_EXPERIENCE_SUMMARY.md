## Critical Success Factor

### âš ï¸ IMPORTANT: Do NOT Over-Animate

**Common mistake:** Adding animation everywhere = feels cheap and slow.

**Premium approach:**
- Each animation has a purpose
- 80% of page is static, 20% is animated
- Animations are between 0.3s-0.8s (not slow)
- Never use cheap easing (always use ease-out, ease-in-out)
- Test on real devices - web feels different than browser
